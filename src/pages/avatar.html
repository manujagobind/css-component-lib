Avatar

Avatars are used to represent user's identity. Avatars come in various shapes and sizes. Here, we have avatar-xl, avatar-lg, avatar-md, and avatar-sm.

Use a div element with an avatar class of your choice to enclose a responsive image. You can use both regular and rounded images. Read more on how to use images here 

                    
    <div class="avatar-xl">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/300/300" alt="Sample avatar" class="img-responsive img-round">
    </div>
    <div class="avatar-lg">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/300/300" alt="Sample avatar" class="img-responsive img-round">
    </div>
    <div class="avatar-md">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/300/300" alt="Sample avatar" class="img-responsive img-round">
    </div>
    <div class="avatar-sm">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/300/300" alt="Sample avatar" class="img-responsive img-round">
    </div>
